### Persona Descriptions

**1. Student Persona — Jordan Lee**  
- **Role:** Undergraduate student enrolled in multiple courses.  
- **Example Scenario:** Jordan logs in before class to check if their Programming Lab assignment has been graded. They quickly see their score, read the professor’s comment, and then click the “Submit” button to upload their next project before the due date.  
- **Goals:** Easily view and submit assignments, check grades, and track attendance from one dashboard.  
- **Frustrations:** Confusing navigation, missing assignment notifications, and difficulty uploading files on time.  
- **Motivation:** Stay organized and maintain strong academic performance by keeping track of deadlines and grades.  
- **How the System Helps:** Provides a clear, mobile-friendly interface showing due dates, grades, and teacher feedback.

---

**2. Teacher Persona — Dr. Andrea Williams**  
- **Role:** Computer Science Instructor managing several classes.  
- **Example Scenario:** Dr. Williams logs into the webpage after class to mark attendance and review student submissions. She uploads an Excel file with grades, provides written feedback, and sends a class announcement about the upcoming midterm.  
- **Goals:** Post assignments, review submissions, grade efficiently, and mark attendance quickly.  
- **Frustrations:** Manual grade entry, repetitive communication with students, and juggling multiple class schedules.  
- **Motivation:** Simplify classroom management and improve communication with students.  
- **How the System Helps:** Enables teachers to upload materials, grade assignments online, and automatically calculate attendance records.

---

**3. Administrator Persona — Ms. Linda Carter**  
- **Role:** School Registrar overseeing system and data accuracy.  
- **Example Scenario:** Ms. Carter logs in at the end of the week to check course enrollment data. She approves two new teacher accounts, updates course information, and exports a student attendance report for the principal.  
- **Goals:** Manage teacher and student accounts, oversee course lists, and generate performance reports.  
- **Frustrations:** Disorganized data, duplicate user accounts, and difficulty tracking course updates.  
- **Motivation:** Maintain an efficient and reliable academic management system.  
- **How the System Helps:** Provides administrative controls to add/remove users, monitor reports, and back up data securely.
